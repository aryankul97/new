Basic Essay Structure

An IELTS essay is composed like any other essay; we just need to make it meaningful. There are three key element to complete an essay :

1.Introduction-The Basic Overview

2.Body Paragraphs-Statements supporting the topic

3.Conclusion-Summarizing it all

Introduction

Make it a note that we only get 40 minutes to write the essay, and some of this time needs to be spent on planning its structure(Maximum of 10 minutes must be spent on planning the structure.This will in a way give the student time to focus on vocabulary and framing sentences). Therefore, we need to be able to write your introduction fairly quickly so that we can start writing your body paragraphs. State the topic of the essay (that you may be able to take from the question)

Body Paragraphs

For an IELTS essay, you should strictly have 2 to 3 body paragraphs only. For your body paragraph, each paragraph should contain one Capturing idea and have sentences to support this.

Conclusion

The conclusion only needs to be one or two sentences.Here you can re-state what the essay is about.Give some thoughts about the future. This completes our Basic Essay Structure.

There are two tasks:

Task 1 - you will be presented with a situation and asked to write a letter requesting information, or explaining the situation. The letter may be personal, semi-formal or formal in style.

Task 2 - you will be asked to write an essay in response to a point of view, argument or problem. The essay can be fairly personal in style. Here We will be discussing about Task-2 only.

IELTS Writing Task 2

Write an academic-style essay on a common topic. You have 40 minutes to write at least 250 words..

Question Analysis

You must first understand the question and read it multiple times to get to the core. Students must answer the questions properly and completely. If student do not answer the question fully, you can't score more than a Band 5.

Planning

The students must plan before they write for up to 10 minutes. Planning helps to organize ideas before you write, saving your time and helping you write a clear and a meaningful essay. Now Write a Simple essay according to the Basic Essay Structure and That's it you're done!
